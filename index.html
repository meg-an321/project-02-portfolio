About Me

I am a born and raised North Carolinian with a deep passion for the intricacies of web development. Having grown up in the charming city of Charlotte, I have always been fascinated by the combination of history, faith, and art that can be found within its borders. This amalgamation of interests has shaped my perspective as a professional, allowing me to approach my work with a unique blend of creativity and precision. As a web developer, I am not only driven by the desire to create visually stunning and user-friendly websites, but also to tell a story through the intersection of design and technology. My commitment to honing my skills and staying at the forefront of industry trends is what truly sets me apart as a developer, and I am excited to continue my journey of combining my passions with my career.
